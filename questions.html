Questions and Negatives in English

Learn, practice, and improve your English skills!

What are Questions?

In English, questions usually start with an auxiliary verb (do, does, are, is) or a Wh- word (what, where, why).
Example: Do you like football?

What are Negatives?

Negatives are made by adding 'not' to auxiliary verbs.
Example: She does not (doesn't) like pizza.

How to form Questions?

Rule: Auxiliary + Subject + Verb
Example: Are you ready?

How to form Negatives?

Rule: Subject + Auxiliary + not + Verb
Example: He is not (isn't) tired.
