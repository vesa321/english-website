Parts of Speech in English

Learn the building blocks of English grammar!

Noun

A word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.
Example: *dog*, *happiness*

Pronoun

A word used instead of a noun.
Example: *he*, *she*, *it*

Verb

A word that shows action or state of being.
Example: *run*, *is*

Adjective

A word that describes a noun or pronoun.
Example: *happy*, *blue*

Adverb

A word that describes a verb, adjective, or another adverb.
Example: *quickly*, *very*

Preposition

Shows relationship between a noun (or pronoun) and other words.
Example: *in*, *on*, *at*

Conjunction

A word that connects words, phrases, or clauses.
Example: *and*, *but*, *or*

Interjection

A word that expresses strong emotion.
Example: *Wow!*, *Oh!*
