📚 Quick Review

The past tense is used to talk about actions that already happened. Let's practice and master it!

📝 Complete the sentences:

1. She ____ (visit) her grandparents last weekend.

2. They ____ (play) soccer after school yesterday.

3. I ____ (watch) a movie on Saturday.

4. My mom ____ (cook) a delicious dinner last night.

5. The students ____ (finish) their homework early.
