Get ready for the future! 🚀

📚 Quick Review

The future tense is used to describe actions that will happen later. Let's practice and prepare! 💬

📝 Complete the sentences:

1. She ____ (travel) to Paris next month.

2. They ____ (visit) their grandparents on Sunday.

3. I ____ (start) a new hobby this year.

4. My friends ____ (join) us at the park later.

5. The teacher ____ (give) us homework tomorrow.
