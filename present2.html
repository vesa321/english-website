📚 Quick Review

The present tense is used to describe actions happening now or facts that are always true. Let's practice together!

📝 Complete the sentences:

1. She ____ (like) to read books every night.

2. They ____ (go) to school by bus.

3. I always ____ (eat) breakfast at 7 AM.

4. My father ____ (work) in a hospital.

5. The sun ____ (rise) in the east.
